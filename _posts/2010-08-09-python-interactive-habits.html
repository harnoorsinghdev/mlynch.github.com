I need to get out of the habit of trying to do everything from the python interactive shell. Even with ipython most of the time I find myself having to re-enter my code over and over until I get it right. It's very frustrating.

Basically, if I need to do a loop, I find it faster to just write a small script than to try to get it right in the REPL.

Not to mention that ipython has a nasty bug that complains about improper indentation levels when recalling a block of code.  The solution is to call %autocall 0.
